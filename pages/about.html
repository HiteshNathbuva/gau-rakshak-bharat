Who We Are

A citizen movement to protect Gau Mata & Bharat's soul

GauRakshak Bharat is a nationwide volunteer initiative uniting farmers, scholars, gaushalas and everyday citizens to safeguard the cow, revive Vedic wisdom, and build a compassionate, self-reliant India — rooted in dharma and driven by action.

Our Story

Why GauRakshak Bharat exists

For millennia, the cow has stood at the heart of Bharat — nourishing families, powering agriculture, and inspiring spiritual life. Yet in a rapidly modernising India, thousands of cows are abandoned, injured or forgotten every day, and the wisdom traditions that once celebrated them are quietly fading.

GauRakshak Bharat was born in 2019 from a simple conviction: Gau Seva is Rashtra Seva. What began as a small group of volunteers in Vrindavan coordinating fodder drives and lawful rescues has grown into a nationwide network of citizens, farmers, gaushalas, scholars and youth — united by dharma and driven by peaceful, on-ground action.

We exist to bridge the ancient and the modern — to bring scriptural wisdom together with modern research, and channel it into education, community outreach and real, tangible protection for every cow of Bharat.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Six timeless principles that shape how we work, whom we welcome, and the Bharat we are helping build together.

Compassion (करुणा)

Every action begins with unconditional care — for the cow, for the community, and for every being that shares Bharat's soil.

Integrity (सत्यनिष्ठा)

Transparent operations, verified information and lawful methods — because dharma cannot be built on shortcuts.

Selfless Service (सेवा)

Nishkama karma — action offered without attachment to reward — is our daily practice and the highest form of worship.

Dharma (धर्म)

Guided by the timeless wisdom of Sanatan tradition, we choose the right path — even when it is the harder one.

Unity (एकता)

Farmer or founder, sanyasi or student — every hand that serves Gau Mata is welcome and equal in this shared yajna.

Sustainability (धरा-रक्षा)

Reviving indigenous breeds, natural farming and panchagavya wisdom for a Bharat that heals its soil, water and air.

Dairy, panchagavya and organic inputs anchor village economies for millions.

Ecological Balance
