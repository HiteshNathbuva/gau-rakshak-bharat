How to Join the Movement

Our simple, structured onboarding pipeline connects you with active district chapters and coordinates verified seva opportunities.

Step 01

Online Registration

Submit a quick 2-minute registration highlighting your district, availability, and specific skills (rescue, education, or support).

Step 02

Identity Verification

Our district coordinators run a standard background and ID verification to ensure community safety and legal alignment.

Step 03

Join Local Team

FAQ

Got Questions? We Have Answers

Find quick, clear information regarding memberships, safety protocols, gaushala support, and donation handling.

Who can join the GauRakshak Bharat community?
Any citizen of India (and supporters globally) who believes in animal welfare, environmental sustainability, and cow protection can join. Volunteers under 18 require parental permission.
Is there a membership fee to join?
No, joining the GauRakshak Bharat community is entirely free. We operate purely on voluntary participation and donation-supported initiatives.
How is my identity verified after registering?
After registering, a local district coordinator will contact you. You will be requested to upload a copy of a government-issued photo ID (such as Aadhaar or Voter ID) to secure verification before official assignments.
Can gaushalas register on the platform?
Yes! Registered gaushala trustees can submit their trust papers, land ownership records, and operational capacities. Once audited and verified, they are added to our national directory to receive veterinary aid, dry-fodder supplies, and volunteer support.
What is the role of a District Coordinator?
District Coordinators manage local volunteer chapters, verify member applications, coordinate animal emergency dispatches, and ensure all local operations adhere to our code of ethics, safety guidelines, and local laws.
How are community donations utilized?
100% of received donations go directly toward buying green/dry fodder, maintaining vet ambulances, funding critical animal surgeries, and developing self-reliant energy setups (like biogas plants) at partner gaushalas. We publish monthly audited accounts.
I have very limited time. Can I still contribute?
Absolutely! You can choose to contribute to digital translation, online design, coordinate emergency updates, sponsor a cow's monthly meal, or participate in weekend-only plantation drives. Every single act of seva counts.
